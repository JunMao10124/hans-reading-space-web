The Three Body Problem: Death's End

By Liu Cixin

Rating: 10/10

What I Loved: I love this book because the story is very big and creative. It talks about space, time, and the future of humans. Some parts were scary but also exciting, like the space war and the universe ending. I learned to think more about how small we are in the universe. This book made me love science fiction even more.

Who I would recommend it to: Anybody who is a sci-fi fan. You 100% won't disappoint after reading this book. I recommend reading all three books.
